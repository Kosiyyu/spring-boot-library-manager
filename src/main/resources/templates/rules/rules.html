Rules


Pretty strict rules:
  • Library users must prominently display their Student ID on them at all times. Students who fail to do so will not be allowed access to the Library facilities.
  • No items belonging to the library are to be taken out of the library unless they have been checked out at the Circulation Desk.
  • Personal belongings should not be left unattended. The library management will not be held responsible for the loss of personal belongings.
  • No food and drink (except plain water) are allowed in the library.
  • Mobile phones or any other personal electronic gadgets must be switched to silent mode before entering the library.
  • Making unreasonable noise, loud conversations, loud cell phone calls or playing loud music or video that can distract other library users in the library is not permitted.
  • Users are prohibited from making or answering calls within the quiet zone.
  • Discussion room is used for academic purposes only and should not be used for private study or social purposes.
  • Eating, drinking, sleeping and smoking are not allowed in the library.
  • The Library users should be professionally attired, as specified in the student handbook. We reserve the right to deny entry to students who are inappropriately attired.
  • Library furniture/equipment should not be moved from its original location.
  • Personal Computers provided are to be strictly used for academic research and Library CD viewing only. These computers cannot be used for personal e-mail, online chatting or playing games.
  • Library staffs reserve the right to inspect bags or other personal property when users enter or leave the library.
  • When a user is caught for breaching the library rules and regulations, immediate action will be taken.
